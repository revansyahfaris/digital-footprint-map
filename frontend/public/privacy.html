PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Digital Footprint Map. This application is an educational student project designed to help users map and analyze their personal digital footprint securely.

1. Data We Access (Google OAuth Scope)

Our application requests access to the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly scope. This permission is used exclusively to scan the metadata (headers, specifically the "From" field) of your emails to identify platforms where you have registered accounts.

2. How We Process and Store Your Data

  • In-Memory Processing: Email contents are analyzed dynamicly in memory and are NEVER read, stored, or saved permanently on our servers.
  • Secure Token Storage: Your Google OAuth access tokens are stored in our database (NeonDB) in a highly encrypted format using industry-standard cryptography (Fernet) to prevent unauthorized access.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information or digital footprint mappings to any third parties.

3. User Control & Data Deletion

You can revoke access to this application at any time through your Google Account security settings. If you log out or request deletion, your encrypted session tokens and footprint data will be wiped from our database immediately.
